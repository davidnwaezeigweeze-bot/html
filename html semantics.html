facts About Dogs

Dogs have a sense of time. It's been proven that they know the difference between an hour and five hours. If conditioned to, they can predict future events, such as regular walk times.

A study was conducted at the University of California, San Diego, where dogs were observed to see if they could understand time intervals. The results showed that dogs were able to anticipate events based on the time elapsed, indicating their ability to perceive time.
